ABOUT OUR JOURNY

Built and developed by an intense passion for calligraphy. With the desire to bring the most useful knowledge about the field of Calligraphy to everyone... Calligraphy is a cultural trait that is not strange to everyone, however, collecting information about calligraphy is extremely difficult thing. Together we have collected, consulted many documents and together created the code data to have a complete calligraphy website with the aim of creating a calligraphy website close to everyone so that learning and Learning about calligraphy will become easy and not as difficult as before. Our aim in creating this calligraphy website is to help people know and better understand calligraphy, its origin, and characteristics. and now we have done it - create a calligraphy website full of information and documents to help people look up and refer.

Success Stories

20 years old guy, 12 years passionate about calligraphy That is Vo Tuan Xuan Thanh, a young student at the University of Fine Arts in Ho Chi Minh City. Ho Chi Minh, also known as the "young man"... Xuan Thanh has had a passion for this very picky art form since he was only 7 years old. During a walk on the old street, the young man was extremely enamored with it. And just like that, Xuan Thanh self-studyed through books, on social networking sites and after only 3 years of practice, at the age of 10, Xuan Thanh won 3rd prize in the program "Green strokes" and that became great motivation to help the guy continue to stick with this passion.
Not only tamed with calligraphy, Xuan Thanh was also the teacher of dozens of students, both big and small. In particular, every time the New Year comes and spring comes, the guy "displays ink and red paper" as a letter-writer for everyone. Not only that, in order to bring calligraphy closer to the public, Xuan Thanh had a very special and creative new experiment when he combined painting and music by writing calligraphy for the lyrics so that he could introduce them to the world. introduce more about calligraphy to everyone.
