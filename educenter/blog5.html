Unique handwriting and reverse calligraphy.

In the field of calligraphy, Hieu Tin is a name that has made many beautiful impressions of professionals and the public. He used to be the chairman of the first calligraphy club of the Youth Cultural House, Ho Chi Minh City. In 2007 was the first person to successfully defend a master's thesis on Vietnamese calligraphy in 2006 with great passion for calligraphy and a spirit of exploration and creativity every year, Hieu Tin produced a series of self-portraits. About the area lying there, for example, the year of his body, creating a cursive shape is the shape of a mischievous monkey in the year of the Rat with a happy mouse, the image of a wise dog for the year of the Dog. The year of the Rabbit is a picture of the cat this year, the New Year of the Ox in 2021, Nguyen Hieu Tuan opens his pen in the beginning of spring with a painting about the year-end zodiac. More unique than that, Nguyen Hieu Tin is one of the few people who has a talent for writing calligraphy, reverses the writing style of Miao Luyen when it is soft and elegant when it is full of sharp inner strength, creating works with a unique personal impression. Constitutional execution in a conventional way requires a lot of practice, but writing backwards requires both technical thinking and real uplifting emotions of the writer. In the midst of modern life, many attractions from high-tech amusements, this 8x teacher's Ong Do restaurant is still preserved in the sparkling memory of the beauty of Vietnamese cultural heritage, many young people are interested in asking for words.
