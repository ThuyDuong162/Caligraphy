Success Stories

Quach Tu Vy is a 12th grader at Bac Lieu High School. She loves to draw ancient paintings, and wants to write some calligraphy on the picture to make it more beautiful. From that idea, I went online to find out how easy it is to write calligraphy. At first, I just wanted to write a few words for fun. However, after delving into this art field, I find it more and more interesting and interesting. "I see calligraphy as a traditional cultural value, but it seems that many young people have forgotten it. So, I wanted to revive it, and then decided to pursue calligraphy seriously, so I tried to learn."
Tu Vy said that she has a dream that after graduating from high school, she will enter architecture or fine arts. However, her passion for painting at first was opposed by her parents. Because both parents are majoring in medicine, they want their children to follow this industry. Although they support their daughter to pursue her passion for painting, Tu Vy's parents still want her to pursue a career in medicine. My mother also advised her daughter to follow the medical profession and consider painting as her "job" on her left hand. However, the daughter with this personality "has the courage" to reply that "if you force me to study medicine, I will study but it will be for my parents, not for me ...". Since then, she has tried to practice calligraphy and successfully realized her passion for calligraphy
